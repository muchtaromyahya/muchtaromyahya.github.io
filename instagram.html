My Goals for the Re-Design :
Increase user comfort in using Instagram especially when exploring the timeline.
Design Instagram to more understand user needs with Empathy.

Observe

Problem Assumptions

1. The Instagram timeline is sorted by popularity.

I realized this problem because when I scrolled through the Instagram timeline, I found a photo that has been uploaded 2 days ago. It caused me to not be able to saw photos that are uploaded by my close friends because it buried by other photos that more popular.

2. It’s so hard to tap usernames that are mentioned in Instagram Story.

When I wanted to open the profile, I a ccidentally moved to the next story or previous story. It always happened to me continuously when I still want to open the profile of the person who is mentioned, until it makes me annoyed.

Persona

For this case I want to focus to design for user types:

Influencer & Content Creator: Users who have a lot of followers and make Instagram as a business platform
Regular Users who use Instagram for entertainment purposes and share a moment with their friends.

User Scenario

User Scenario

I asked my friends to tried to tap the username that is mentioned on someone’s Instagram Story and I asked them to checked the content of their timeline. The result is

Users can’t always tap the username. The thing that happened was they are moved to the next or previous story. One participant also got a problem because she uses a 5inc smartphone.

Contents of their timeline mostly aren’t from their friends, but from an artist, influencer or online shop that they have followed. Even post has been uploaded 2 days ago is still in their timeline.

Define

After did some Observes, surprisingly, I got a new interesting problem that has to be solved

Pain Points #1: It’s so hard to tap usernames that are mentioned in Instagram Story.
Pain Points #2: The Instagram timeline is sorted by popularity.
This is a new problem that I got when I did an interview.
Pain Points #3: Forget to upload photo or video

Ideate

Before I made Hi-Fi User Interface, I decided to sketch the design on paper first to minimize material and feature are forgotten. This method makes me faster and more effective to find a solution, and also speeds up when making Hi-Fi versions.

Prototype

High Fidelity

Flow #1: An easier way to tap the username/hashtag that is mentioned on Instagram Story.
Flow #2: How to set the content of Instagram timeline based on upload time, popularity and your close friends.
Flow #3: How to set your post upload schedule on Instagram
